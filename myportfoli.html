About Me


I'm Adigun Zainab Ewaoluwa, a Computer and Electronics Engineering student at Lagos State University,
passionate about building with both hardware and code.
I'm currently developing my skills in web development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) and UI/UX design,
and I enjoy creating projects that solve real problems and look good doing it.
Throughout my academic journey, I have had the opportunity to work on various projects,
including an loT-based embedded robotic car and a business navigation website, in addition to this portfolio.
These experiences have provided me with valuable insights into how technology can enhance our daily lives and professional practices.
Outside tech, I run an accessories brand "Glam Me Accessories" where I design and create stylish,
handmade pieces. Whether I'm coding, designing, or creating something physical, I love bringing ideas to life.
I'm constantly learning, building, and growing and I'm excited for what's ahead.
